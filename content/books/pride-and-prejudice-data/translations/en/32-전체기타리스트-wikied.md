## A. Legal and Economic Institutions

| # | English | Designation | Definition | Function in the Novel | Details |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

| 2 | Settlement | Marriage Settlement | An agreement over property made as part of a marriage contract | Central to the terms of Lydia and Wickham's marriage | [Institutional/Economic Terms] |
