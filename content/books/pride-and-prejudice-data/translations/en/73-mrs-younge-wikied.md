# Mrs. Younge

## Basic Information
- **Original name**: Mrs. Younge
- **Korean rendering**: Mrs. Younge / Younge
- **Position**: Former companion and governess to Georgiana Darcy
- **Residence**: London (later the proprietress of a lodging house)
- **First mentioned**: chunk_10 (Chapter 35, Darcy’s letter) — EVT-029
- **Reappears**: chunk_15 (Chapter 46 onward) — EVT-042

## Character Profile
Formerly Georgiana Darcy’s companion and governess, Mrs. Younge conspired with Wickham to seduce Georgiana and persuade her to elope. She was dismissed when the scheme was discovered and later ran a lodging house in London. During the Lydia–Wickham elopement, Darcy seeks Mrs. Younge out and pays her for information that enables him to trace Wickham.

## Core Relationships
- **Wickham**: Her accomplice. She participated in the scheme to seduce Georgiana and later remained informed of Wickham’s whereabouts
- **Georgiana Darcy**: Her former charge, whose trust she betrayed
- **Darcy**: Dismissed her after the Ramsgate plot; during Lydia’s crisis, he contacts her again and pays her to reveal Wickham’s whereabouts
