# Mr. Jones

## Basic Information
- **Original name**: Mr. Jones
- **Korean rendering**: Mr. Jones / Jones
- **Occupation**: Apothecary
- **Residence**: Meryton
- **First appearance**: chunk_02 (Chapters 7–8)

## Character Profile
The apothecary of Meryton, who attends Jane when she falls ill at Netherfield. In middle-class households of the period, an apothecary rather than a physician commonly provided primary medical care, and Mr. Jones performs that role. He is a functional character with almost no direct speech or characterization.

## Core Relationships
- **Jane Bennet**: Treats her during her illness with a cold
- **Mrs. Bennet**: Receives news of Jane’s condition through Mr. Jones’s diagnosis
