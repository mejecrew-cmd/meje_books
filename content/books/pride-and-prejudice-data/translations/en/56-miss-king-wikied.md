# Miss King

## Basic Information
- **Original name:** Miss King
- **Korean rendering:** Miss King
- **Fortune:** inherited £10,000 from her grandfather
- **Residence:** near Meryton
- **First appearance:** chunk_07 (Chap. 26)
- **Related event:** EVT-022, Wickham changes the object of his courtship

## Character Introduction
A young woman who inherits £10,000 from her grandfather. Wickham abandons his interest in Elizabeth and turns his courtship toward Miss King. She functions as a narrative device exposing Wickham’s practical—or opportunistic—character and scarcely appears in person.

## Core Relationships
- **Wickham:** courts her with an eye to her inheritance
- **Elizabeth:** indirectly connected through Wickham’s change of interest; Elizabeth initially accepts it calmly, but it later becomes a clue to Wickham’s true nature
