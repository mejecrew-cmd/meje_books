# Mr. Hurst

## Basic Information
- **Original name**: Mr. Hurst
- **Korean rendering**: Mr. Hurst / Hurst
- **Status**: Gentry; Bingley’s brother-in-law
- **Residence**: Staying at Netherfield Park and living with Bingley
- **First appearance**: chunk_01 (Chapter 3)

## Character Profile
Mrs. Hurst’s husband and Bingley’s brother-in-law. His presence is so faint that “merely looked the gentleman” virtually exhausts his description. He has no interests beyond eating, drinking, and playing cards, and remains a background figure with scarcely any meaningful speech or action in the entire novel.

## Core Relationships
- **Mrs. Hurst**: His wife; accompanies the Bingley siblings in their social activities
- **Bingley**: His brother-in-law; stays with him at Netherfield
- **Miss Bingley**: His sister-in-law; accompanies her socially
