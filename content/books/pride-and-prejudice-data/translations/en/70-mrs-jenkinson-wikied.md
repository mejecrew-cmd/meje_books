# Mrs. Jenkinson

## Basic Information
- **Original name**: Mrs. Jenkinson
- **Korean rendering**: Mrs. Jenkinson / Jenkinson
- **Position**: Companion and governess to Miss de Bourgh (Anne)
- **Residence**: Rosings Park
- **First appearance**: chunk_08 (Chapter 29)

## Character Profile
Anne de Bourgh’s constant companion, whose principal duty is to watch over and attend to what Miss de Bourgh eats at meals. She is said to have a pianoforte in her room—Lady Catherine offers to let Elizabeth practice there—and remains a background figure who exists like Anne’s shadow.

## Core Relationships
- **Miss de Bourgh (Anne)**: Her charge, whom she constantly attends
- **Lady Catherine**: Her employer; cares for Anne under Lady Catherine’s direction
