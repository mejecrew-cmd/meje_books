# Mr. Denny

## Basic Information
- **Original name**: Mr. Denny
- **Korean rendering**: Mr. Denny / Denny
- **Position**: Militia officer
- **Residence**: Stationed at Meryton
- **First appearance**: chunk_04 (Chapter 15)
- **Related event**: EVT-013 (Wickham’s first appearance)

## Character Profile
A militia officer stationed at Meryton who brings Wickham from London and introduces him to the Bennet sisters. He serves as Wickham’s point of entry into Meryton society and is occasionally mentioned afterward as Wickham’s fellow officer.

## Core Relationships
- **Wickham**: Brings him from London and helps him join the militia; the direct intermediary for Wickham’s entrance
- **Lydia and Kitty**: Socializes with them at gatherings of the officers
