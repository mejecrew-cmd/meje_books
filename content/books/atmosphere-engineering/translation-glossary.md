## Style rules

- Preserve the original heading hierarchy and paragraph order.
- Do not convert explanatory claims into stronger scientific claims.
- Preserve film, game, scholar, and model names in their established target-language forms.
- Keep numeric values, Kelvin values, ratios, and layer counts unchanged.
- Translate examples, but do not localize or replace the referenced works.
- Use direct, instructional prose. Avoid marketing embellishment.
- Chinese Traditional and Simplified are separate translations, not automated script conversions.
